FCTC-Air

FCTC-Air is a educational project. The purpose is to build a distributed air quality monitoring system that everyone can access. "FCTC" is the abbreviation of "From Chip To Cloud", means that involve all stack of IoT design, including embeded software, web, app, and so on. As well as help people pay attention to air pollution.
